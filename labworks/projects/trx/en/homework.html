Methodical instructions

Spectral density of noise power, calculated at the input of receiver, can be calculated by the next formula:

N0 = k(kn-1)T0, W/Hz

where k = 1,38·10 -23 W/(Hz · K) - Botzman constant;
kn – receiver noise coefficient in relative units;
T0 – receiver temperature in Kelvin degrees. Receiver temperature is given.

Transition from relative to logarithmic units and back is carried out by the formulas:

k,dB = 10 lg k;

k = 10k,dB / 10.

To calculate the noise power at the receiver's input of the mobile station, the next expression can be used:

Pn = N0 Δ Frec, W

where N0 - spectral density of noise power,
Δ Frec – receiver frequency bandwidth. Value N0 should be substituted in W/Hz, frequency bandwidth Δ Frec in hertz.
